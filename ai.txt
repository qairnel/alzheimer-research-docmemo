# Alzheimer Research - Clinical Trial Matching

Free online memory questionnaire connecting people to Alzheimer's clinical trials in California and Florida.

## What we do
- Free memory assessment questionnaire (10 minutes)
- Match people with clinical trials based on their profile
- Connect to local research centers
